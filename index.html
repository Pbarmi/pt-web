My Beauty My Standards

Hi everyone, in today’s topic I am discussing a somewhat controversial topic around Asian Women’s beauty standards. Now, this probably is an alien topic to those who don’t know what I am talking about and to others it is that topic that is never ending that constantly is what we hear hours about.

I am a 20-year-old, British Sikh woman living in the UK. I have tattoos, I have short hair, I have piercings I am not skinny, I am not tall. How does that make you feel? I literally could be an Auntyji’s nightmare really. I hear comments all the time saying; “Oh wow, she has tattoos and she isn’t even married yet”, “Her piercings are disgusting *chichichi”, “Her skin isn’t fair enough she needs to lighten it” and finally, the classic question, “What would your future mother-in-law and your in laws think? You name it, I have heard it.
